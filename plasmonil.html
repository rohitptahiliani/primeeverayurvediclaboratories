Cap. Plasmonil

A Novel Cure for Malaria

Dosage Form Capsule

Indications

Cap. Plasmonil is indicated for the Treatment as well as Prophylaxis of Malaria cases (P. Vivax and P. Falciparum). It is specifically indicated and beneficial for:

  • Multi Drug Resistant P. Falciparum Malaria
  • Frequent Relapses of P. Vivax Malaria
  • Intermittent Preventive Therapy during Pregnancy (IPTp)
  • Frequently recurring Malaria in children

Distinct Advantages

  • Purely Herbo-Mineral drug — does not contain any harmful metal or heavy metal.
  • No adverse effects. No drug interactions.
  • Rapidly acting drug — Fever Clearance Time: 30 to 48 hours only.
  • Kills the malarial parasite (Plasmodium) through the immune system of the body; chances of resistance developing are almost nil.
  • Stimulates the Bone Marrow and increases Platelet count, White cell and Red cell count.
  • Normalizes the disturbed Rheology of blood and restores Micro circulation.
  • Very safe for children and in conditions like Pregnancy, G6PD deficiency, Blood/CVS/CNS disorders, Hypersensitivity, Malaria associated with Viral Hepatitis or other Liver/Kidney disorders.
